About Levin Keller

Dear visitor, my Name is Levin Keller, I am 30 years old and fell down the Bitcoin rabbit hole.

I believe that blockchain-based technology will change our society for the better and work on spreading the word, teaching people about Bitcoin and enable them to use it as much as they can.

On this page you will find links to my social network profiles, projects that I am working on and ways to get in touch.

Projects I'm currently involved with:

  • Bundesverband Bitcoin e.V. - board member.
  • Satoshipay - technical adviser.
